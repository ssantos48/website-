Born: November 26, 1992

Labels: Real Hasta La Muerte; Sony Latin

Biography

Emmanuel Gazmey Santiago mostly known as "Anuel AA". He is a very well known Reggaeton artist, he started recording music at the age of 14 and began posting it online. His 2016 mixtape Real Hasta La Muerte was well received, but his success was put on hold the same year by a 30-month prison sentence for illegal firearm possession in Puerto Rico. He recorded the entire album while being incarcerated. Due to his album "Real Hasta La Muerte" being so successful he appeared on the Billboard Hot Latin Song. He recorded a lot of music videas with many other famous Reggaeton singers. He has 5 studio albums, 36 singles, 1 mixtape, and 3 promotional singles. Over the years Anuel AA has been a very successful reggaeton singer.
